The Library of Babel

An endless library you can walk. It is made of hexagonal galleries — each with four walls of bookshelves (700 books per gallery) and two hallways that open onto the next gallery, plus a staircase up and down.

Nothing here is stored. Every gallery and every book is generated on the spot from its coordinates — so the same place always holds exactly the same books, forever, for everyone. The Library is infinite, yet it weighs almost nothing: only your path is remembered.

How to wander

  • ◁ ▷ hallways · ▲ ▼ stairs — or use the arrow keys.
  • Click any colored spine to read a book. Inside, arrow keys turn its 410 pages.
  • borrow book downloads the full book (~1.3 MB) as a .txt.
  • export saves your journey; new walk drops you somewhere random.

The numbers in the header

gallery (z, n) is your coordinate. hash is a fingerprint of this gallery's 700 books — a permalink and proof, identical every time you return. Your last 50 galleries are kept in full; every step's hash is kept forever.
